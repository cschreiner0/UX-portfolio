ABOUT

Hello! My name is Carrie and I enjoy doing user experience research and design. As an enthusiastic, hardworking and organized person, I take pride in improving tools and products in order to better the lives of others. I have worked with others from many countries, backgrounds, and cultures along with able-diverse colleagues. This has influenced my research and designs to include accessible solutions for both local, able-diverse, generalized, and global audiences. I have experience working both remotely and in-person on projects ranging from large teams to individual projects.

When I have free time, you can find me hiking, biking, reading, or playing card, dice, board, TTRPG like DnD, or video games. I also enjoy actively learning new skills, playing with my cats, hanging out with others and crafting woodwork. The two new skills that I am learning are installing electrical work in a house and knitting.
